Seasonal Escapes Website – Site Plan

Site Name

Seasonal Escapes

This name reflects a platform dedicated to showcasing trips that change throughout the year, helping users discover local tours and seasonal events.

Optional domain: seasonalescapes.com.mx

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to offer travelers curated seasonal tours, monthly activities, and local travel deals. It will provide detailed tour descriptions, pricing, itineraries, and contact information for bookings or inquiries.

Scenarios

  • What seasonal tours are available this month?
  • How can I contact a tour representative to ask questions or reserve a spot?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Teal (#6FB09A) — Used for headings and accents.

Secondary Color: Sand Beige (#F6F5F0) — Used for page background.

Typography

Fonts: Poppins & Montserrat

  • Headings: Montserrat – bold (modern, clean, and strong visual hierarchy)
  • Body text: Poppins – regular (smooth and highly readable)
